My Recipe

1. Take some maida and make a dough by adding some milk, baking powder
2. Apply some oil on the dough prepared and let it sit for 30 minutes by covering it in a plastic
3. After 30 minutes, the dough would be puffed 
4. shape the dough into the size of your choice and make some small chops using fork spoon
5. Apply the pizza sauce, veggies of your choice and top with the cheese
6. Microwave it for 20 minutes and pizza is ready
